Petugas PRU ke - 15

On November 19, 2022, the country went to the polls for the 15th General Election. Because of this, I decided to offer my services as a volunteer to the SPR officer who is in charge of this PRU.
At that time, I was working as a guide, and one of my responsibilities was to demonstrate to voters where they should cast their ballots.
The fact that there are so many various kinds of individuals makes it both incredibly exhausting and quite enjoyable.
In addition, in order to be a GE officer, one needs to have trust, and they are not allowed to leave their job station at will. Work duties were assigned to me at SMK Melawati.
